Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

At NoSaveWA, your privacy is our absolute priority. This policy outlines how we handle data when you use our application.

1. Zero Data Collection

NoSaveWA operates as a completely client-side application. We do not collect, store, track, transmit, or sell your phone numbers, messages, or metadata. All data inputs are processed locally in your browser.

2. Local Storage Usage

NoSaveWA uses your browser's local storage (LocalStorage) purely for your convenience to maintain a list of your recent chats. This data never leaves your device. You can clear your history at any time using the "Clear All" button.

3. Client-Side Utilities

All QR codes and link generations are computed locally on your device using JavaScript. No external API calls are made to process your phone numbers or messages.

4. Security & Hosting

The application is securely hosted on Vercel via encrypted HTTPS connection. We do not use advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party tracking pixels.
